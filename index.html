Mariana Neves

Currently, I am a researcher at the ZEBET in the Bundesinstitut für Risikobewertung (BfR) (Berlin, Germany). I obtained my doctoral degree in Computer Science at the Universidad Complutense de Madrid (Madrid, Spain) and my Master Science degree in Computer Science at Universidade Federal de Pernambuco (Recife, Brazil). I graduated in Civil Engineering also at the Universidade Federal de Pernambuco.
